About

[ photo ]

I'm a junior at the University of Chicago studying Physics and Mathematics, with a focus on applied engineering — from spacecraft structures to high-energy laser physics.

My work spans structural design and FEA for the Pulse-A CubeSat program, experimental physics research at SLAC National Accelerator Laboratory, and the intersection of rigorous analysis with hands-on fabrication. I'm drawn to problems where precision matters and failure isn't an option.

Outside the lab and machine shop, I'm interested in aerospace systems, inertial confinement fusion, and the engineering challenges that sit at the edge of what's currently possible.
